## Why This Skill Was Retired

Party and role identification — for every role across every contract in the
project body, including the Engineer — belongs entirely to the Compliance SME.

Identifying who holds the Engineer role requires reading across the
construction contract, the PMC appointment, the consultancy agreement, and
any delegation letters. This is governance establishment work, not legal
analysis. A legal SME characterises what a role is authorised to do under
the governing contract standard. The Compliance SME establishes who actually
holds that role, under what instrument, from what date, confirmed by what
document.

  from what date, with what authority confirmed by what document
